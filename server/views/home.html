Check the controls for exporting your waste

You can use this service to find out what controls apply to exporting your waste to a specific country.

This service is not intended to help you classify your waste. It is your responsibility to ensure that your waste is appropriately classified.

This service only covers the waste types listed in Annexes III and IV of the Waste Shipments Regulation. There are many wastes that are not included in these Regulations. If you cannot find the waste that you want to export you must describe it as ‘not listed’ and notification controls will apply.

Please read our guidance webpage for full information about waste shipments.
