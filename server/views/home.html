Check the controls for exporting your waste

You can use this service to find out what controls apply to exporting your waste from England, Wales, Scotland, or Northern Ireland to the country it’s destined for.

You should not use this service to classify your waste. It is your responsibility to classify your waste correctly. Further information on waste classification is available here:

This service only covers the waste types listed in Annexes III and IV of the Waste Shipments Regulation. There are many wastes not included in these Regulations. If you cannot find the waste you want to export you must describe it as ‘not listed’ and notification controls will apply.

Please read our guidance webpage for full information about waste shipments.
